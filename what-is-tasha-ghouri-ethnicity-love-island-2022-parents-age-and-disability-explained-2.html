Tasha Ghouri Disability Explained

The Love Island competitor Tasha Ghouri has difficulty hearing and is a clinically deaf person. She was first discovered to be deaf by her parents when she was just an infant.

Moreover, Tasha has been wearing a cochlear hearing implant since the early age of five. It has enabled her to hear things to an extent. She has revealed that she changed her implant at the age of 16 and did not put it in her both ears when she had an option because she is used to hearing with only one ear.

It was also revealed that Tasha does not need an interpreter in the show, as she is able to hear by herself to an extent. Her inclusion on the show has been praised by Royal National Institute for Deaf People.
