The Queen of Shebas Sword, Kingdom Come: Deliverance Quest

Publish date: 2024-04-11

1. Collect together the five pieces of the Queen of Sheba’s sword and bring them to the swordsmith in Sasau.

I'll have to talk to the others swordsmiths and blacksmiths who bought the pieces as talismans and try to get them from them.

#1

One of the pieces can only be acquired during the Aquarius quest in Rattay. You can get it by assigning Pickman work as a water carrier.

#2

The blacksmith in Sasau, named Zach, has another part of the sword. You can either pickpocket him, or knock him down and steal it.

#3

This piece of the sword is in a trunk on the second floor of Rattay swordsmith's home. You can steal his key or pick the very hard lock to get it.

#4

Another piece of the sword can be bought for 200 groshen or stolen from the blacksmith in Ledetchko.

#5

This piece is lying on a barrel inside the blacksmith's house in Talmberg.

I finally managed to get all the pieces of the sword. The swordsmith was delighted and pay the promised reward. And he told me I should come next day and see the reforged sword.
