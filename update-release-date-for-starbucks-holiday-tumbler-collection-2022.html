Update: Release date for Starbucks Holiday Tumbler Collection 2022

Publish date: 2024-07-29

Update: Release date for Starbucks Holiday Tumbler Collection 2022-The Starbucks holiday collection of reusable cups and tumblers for 2022 features red, dark blue, mint green, gold, sangria, and other colors, as well as designs like ornaments, woodland creatures, poinsettias, and lace. Some of the collection may already be in cafés, while the rest is expected to arrive in stores on different dates in November.

Does Starbucks have holiday cups 2022?

Yes, Starbucks has holiday cups for 2022. Starbucks designers dialed up the joy with this year’s collection of gift-inspired holiday cup designs. The 2022 red cups come with holiday motifs of Frosted Sparkles, Gift-Wrapped Magic, Ornament Wonder, and Cozy Evergreen. You can also find new Jeweled Cold Cups available in white, merlot and azure.

What Starbucks cups are coming out 2022?

Starbucks has released its 2022 holiday cup designs which are gift-inspired and feature holiday motifs of Frosted Sparkles, Gift-Wrapped Magic, Ornament Wonder, and Cozy Evergreen1

. You can read more about the designs on the Starbucks website.

Are Starbucks holidays cups out yet?

Yes, the Starbucks holiday cups and seasonal beverages became available on November 4, 2021.

How many Starbucks holiday cups are there?

There are four holiday cups in store this year and they were designed to look like petite presents, with a red, green, and white color scheme.

How can I get a Starbucks Holiday Cup?

You can get a free reusable holiday cup from Starbucks when you buy a holiday drink on Thursday. Customers who order a holiday or fall hand-crafted beverage will receive a free, limited-edition reusable red cup. Starbucks says the cups are available while supplies last. Customers can get their free cup by ordering from their nearby Starbucks, via delivery or using the Starbucks app.
