American Idol Finale on Sunday May 21

Publish date: 2024-07-10

American Idol performers list: Who’s performing at the American Idol finale 2023?

After a long journey for us, but especially the contestants, we’re finally going to find out who is the next American Idol winner! The American Idol 2023 season finale airs tonight on ABC. Who are you voting for?

To tune in, make sure you’ve freed up your night because the episode is an extra long one! The finale begins at 8 p.m. ET and runs for three hours. During that time, the Top 3 contestants will be mentored by Keith Urban, and will give us performances for the last time to secure our live votes at home.

While of course Colin Stough, Iam Tongi, and Megan Danielle will be taking the Idol stage, the exciting night also has a slew of artists lined up to keep us entertained throughout the night. Who can you expect to see? We’ve got the answer for you below!

Who is performing at the American Idol finale 2023?

While Keith Urban makes his way back to the ABC series to share his knowledge (he was a judge on seasons 12 through 15), Urban is also going to be performing. Idol judges Katy Perry, Lionel Richie, and Luke Bryan also move away from the judging panel for a little bit and take the stage. Some of “today’s top artists” and “music legends” will be celebrating the finale, per ABC. More surprises are to come, but here’s who we know will be there so far:

Clay Aiken & Ruben Studdard Ellie Goulding James Blunt Jazmine Sullivan Jelly Roll Keith Urban (also mentoring Top 3) Kevin Cronin from REO Speedwagon Kylie Minogue Lainey Wilson Lauren Daigle Pitbull TLC

Judges Katy Perry, Lionel Richie, and Luke Bryan

The 2023 season’s Top 12 return for a performance

Plus, we have some great news! The network has already renewed the singing series for another season. That means we can expect to see American Idol season 22 sometime in 2024! Stay tuned to Hidden Remote as we bring you updates about the official premiere date and other details about the season.

The American Idol 2023 season finale airs Sunday, May 21, 2023 on ABC.
