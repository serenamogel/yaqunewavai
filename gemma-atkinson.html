Gemma Louise Atkinson (born on 16 November 1984) in Bury, Greater Manchester, England, United Kingdom. She is an English actress, writer, radio presenter, and ex glamour model.

Career

Atkinson is best known for her role in Hollyoaks (2001–2005, TV series). Its three sequels are Hollyoaks: After Hours (2004), Hollyoaks: Let Loose (2005), and Hollyoaks: In the City (2006). She has also appeared in Casualty (2011–2012, 2014, TV series).

In recent years, she starred in Emmerdale (2015–2017, TV series). She took her General Certificate of Secondary Education at Castlebrook High School, Bury. She appeared in the horror film 13Hrs (2010). Atkinson has also performed the lead role in the comedy film How to Stop Being a Loser (2011).

In 2007, she took part in the Celebrity MasterChef competition. Also in 2007, she appeared in the seventh season of I’m a Celebrity…Get Me Out of Here! (2002) in Australia. She will publish her The Ultimate Body Plan for New Mums: 12 Weeks to Finding You Again, fitness and recipe book.

In 2018, she published The Ultimate Body Plan, a cookbook and diet book. Atkinson has a huge fan following on her Instagram account where she often shares her baby pictures and lifestyles. She also shares her workout videos with her fans.

She made her appearance as Lt. Eva McKenna in the video game Command & Conquer: Red Alert 3 (2008) and reprised in Command & Conquer Red Alert 3: Uprising (2009).
