Who is Adam D’Angelo and What is his Net Worth?

Adam D’Angelo is an American engineer and developer who has a net worth of $700 million dollars. He was an early employee of Facebook and the founder of Quora, a question-and-answer website that enables users to create, edit, and organize its content. D’Angelo attended Phillips Exeter Academy and the California Institute of Technology, where he participated in programming contests and developed an AIM buddy list website called BuddyZoo.

As one of Mark Zuckerberg’s oldest friends, D’Angelo was well-positioned to take on the role of Facebook’s CTO in its earliest stages. He later served as Vice President of Engineering. In 2008, D’Angelo left Facebook and co-founded Quora with another former Facebook employee, Charlie Cheever. Quora requires users to register with their real names and allows them to link their accounts to Twitter and Facebook. D’Angelo also served as an advisor and investor in Instagram before it was acquired by Facebook.
