Famous for her role in the series Itaewon Class, actress Lee Joo Young was born in Seoul, South Korea. She was raised alongside her sibling, about whom not much is known. The same is the case with her parents as well.

As for her education, young Lee went to Changhyeon High School and later enrolled at the Kyung Hee University-Theatre and Film. She completed her bachelors’ degree from there.

Lee Joo Young is the first actress to play a transgender role.

Lee Joo Young is a remarkable actress who got fame for her role in the recent series, Itaewon Class.

What made it impactful was that Lee played the role of Ma Hyun Yi, a transgender character. She talked about playing such a huge character during her shooting with Marie Claire, Korea.

Similarly, Joo Young was featured in Marie Claire’s video project titled “Gender Free Season 3,” which features actresses performing gender-bound roles. Lee talked about her approach to the role.

“I thought a lot about how I should play the role. I decided that rather than dwelling on gender while acting, should act as the character Ma Hyun Yi himself.”

Aside from Itaewon Class, the young actress is also recognized for playing tomboyish roles. She has appeared in some remarkable series like Weightlifting Fairy Kim Bok Joo(2016-17), Something in the Rain(2018), The Ghost Detective(2018), Drama Special(2019), and more.

Likewise, many might not know, but Lee made her debut way back in 2011. She entered the entertainment industry from the short film titled Behinds in 2011, followed by Encounter, Living Things, Beauty of Journey, and A Hidden Story.

In her early years, Lee Joo Young kept working in short films such as The Transfer Student (2015) and Alone Together(2017).

But Lee Joo Young starred in a silver-screen movie, A Quiet Dream as Joo Young in 2016. That same year, the rising actress starred as Jisu in a movie titled Jane. 

Moreover, in 2020, Lee starred in another stellar movie titled Baseball Girl. Joo Young played a girl named Soo In, a high school graduated who was set on being a professional baseball player.

“At first, Soo In seemed like a reckless person to me. As someone in my 20s, I did wish that Soo In would find a path that was a bit easier, but it turned out that Soo In wasn’t reckless, she was just very certain about her dream.”

To this added, “It was just that she wanted to be a professional athlete, rather than wanting to play baseball as a hobby or teach it.”
