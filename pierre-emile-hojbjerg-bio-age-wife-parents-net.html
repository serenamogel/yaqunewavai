Pierre-Emile Højbjerg biography

Pierre-Emile Kordt Højbjerg is a professional Danish footballer who plays as a midfielder in the English Premier League.

Højbjerg started his professional career when he was 17 years old. He joined FC Bayern Munich II for two seasons before sticking to the main team. However, he made simultaneous appearances for both the Academy and the Senior team before becoming a full member of the main team.

He was with the FC Bayern Munich team for 4 years which includes 2 years on loan at 2 different Bundesliga clubs, FC Augsburg and Schalke 04. He made a total of 115 appearances and scored 14 goals throughout his stay at Germany before his departure.

Højbjerg came to the English Premier League in 2016-2017 when he joined Southampton. He spent 4 amazing seasons at Southampton and made a total of 134 appearances and scored only 4 goals.

Pierre-Emile Højbjerg career

The Tottenham Hotspur star has made a really impressive career for himself so far. He has made a total of 380 appearances and scored 28 goals. He has also won the Bundesliga and DFB-Pokal twice each.
