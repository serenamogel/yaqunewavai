Spirit Break (Japanese: ソウルクラッシュ Soul Crash) is a damage-dealing Fairy-type move introduced in Generation VIII. Prior to Generation IX, it was one of the signature moves of Grimmsnarl.

Effect

Spirit Break inflicts damage and lowers the target's Special Attack stat by one stage.

Generation VIII

Pokémon Brilliant Diamond and Shining Pearl

Spirit Break cannot be selected in a battle.
