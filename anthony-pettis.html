Biography

Anthony Pettis was born in Milwaukee, WI on January 27, 1987. Lightweight American mixed martial artist who began his World Extreme Cagefighting career in 2009 and joined the UFC in 2010 with the WEC/UFC merger. For much of his career, he was ranked as a Top Ten pound-for-pound fighter but lost that status in 2017 after four successive losses. He defeated Jeremy Stephens in a split decision match on October 8, 2011, at UFC 136. He attended Dominican High School and studied boxing and tae kwon do.
